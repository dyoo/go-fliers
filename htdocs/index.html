WPI Go

The WPI Go group meets weekly on Wednesday evenings, starting from 7pm in the Campus Center. We're usually at the tables right next to the Dunkin Donuts.

History

  • Beginner's Night (2009/02/20)

    Beginner's Night on Friday, February 20, 2009 at 7pm, on the 2nd floor of the Campus Center. Beginners are most welcome! Come by and learn the rules of the game and meet other people.
