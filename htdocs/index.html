The WPI Go Club

The WPI Go group meets weekly on Monday evenings, starting from 7pm at the Mid-Century room in the Campus Center till about 9pm (sometimes later). Please feel free to come by; we welcome people of all skill levels!

History

  • Inter-team competition (2010/03/27)

    We competed in the Inter-team competition hosted by the MGA. Participants from WPI were: David Saxner, Ben Timms, and Danny Yoo. We won two out of our four games.

  • Beginners Night (2009/11/11)

    Stop by and learn to play Go if you don't know how. It only takes 10 minutes to learn and will provide a lifetime of enjoyment. If you already know how to play stop by and play a few games, we have people of all different levels so you are sure to find someone else your level. There will be drinks and snacks provided.
    Venue: WPI Campus Center, near the Dunkin Donuts, 7-10pm.

  • Beginner's Night (2009/02/20)

    Beginner's Night on Friday, February 20, 2009 at 7pm, on the 2nd floor of the Campus Center. Beginners are most welcome! Come by and learn the rules of the game and meet other people.
